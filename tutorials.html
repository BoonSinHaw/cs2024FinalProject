How to Use Lumeva Like a Pro

Follow these simple steps for best results — it’s easier than you think.

Watch: 2-Minute Quick Start Guide

Step-By-Step Instructions

1

Prepare Your Skin

Shave the area you plan to treat. Clean and dry completely — no lotion or deodorant residue.

2

Power On & Select Intensity

Turn on your device and let the Smart Skin Sensor detect your tone. Adjust to your comfort level (1–5).

3

Flash & Glide

Hold the window flush against your skin. Press the flash button and move slowly across the area — don’t overlap flashes.

4

Repeat Consistently

Use once every 2 weeks for the first 8 weeks.
After that, maintain every 4–6 weeks.

5

Care After Use

Apply a soothing aloe gel or moisturizer. Avoid
direct sun exposure for 24 hours.

Your Comfort Comes First

Not for use on tattoos or moles

Avoid on recently tanned or sunburned skin

Always wear protective eyewear if treating facial areas

Troubleshooting

Q: “It’s not flashing — why?”

A: Ensure full skin contact; the device won’t trigger unless securely pressed.

Q: “Can I use it every day?”

A: No. Overuse doesn’t increase results — stick to your recommended schedule.
