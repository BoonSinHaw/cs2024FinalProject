Bringing Life Through Light

Lumeva was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel confident in her own skin, without compromise.

Our Beginning

Lumeva was founded in 2022 by Dr. Elena Chen, a biomedical engineer, and Sarah Tan, a beauty industry veteran. Both women shared a frustration: the lack of accessible, safe, and effective hair removal solutions designed specifically for Asian skin tones.

After years of expensive salon treatments and disappointing at-home devices, they decided to create something better. Something that would bring professional-grade results into every home, making salon-quality confidence accessible to all women.

The Lumeva Difference

The name “Lumeva” comes from the Latin “Lumen” (light) and “Eva” (life), symbolizing our mission to bring life and confidence through light technology. We spent three years in research and development, working with dermatologists, engineers, and hundreds of test users across Southeast Asia.

Our breakthrough came with the development of LightPulse™ Technology, a proprietary IPL system that’s specifically calibrated for Asian skin tones. Combined with our smart skin sensor and adjustable intensity levels, we created a device that’s not only effective but also incredibly safe and easy to use.
