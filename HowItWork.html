What Is IPL?

IPL stands for Intense Pulsed Light. It’s a gentle yet powerful technology that uses multiple wavelengths of light to target the melanin in your hair follicles. When the light energy is absorbed, it converts into gentle heat — putting hair follicles into a resting phase. Over time, regrowth becomes lighter, finer, and eventually stops altogether.

It’s the same clinically proven technology used in beauty salons — now miniaturized into a safe, portable home device.
