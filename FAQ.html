IPL Safety & Effectiveness

01
Is IPL safe for all skin tones?
Yes. LumeaGlow’s Smart Sensor adjusts light intensity based on your skin tone for safe and comfortable treatment. It’s not recommended for very dark skin or very light blonde/grey hair (low melanin).
02
Does IPL hurt?
Not at all. Most users describe it as a warm flick on the skin. The cooling tip ensures every flash feels gentle and painless.
03
How soon will I see results?
You’ll notice visible hair reduction in 4–6 weeks, and smoother, longer-lasting results after 8–12 weeks of consistent use.
04
Can I use it on my face?
Yes, for women it’s safe below the cheekbones (upper lip, chin, jawline). Avoid the eye area and always use the included protective eyewear.
05
Is IPL permanent?
Results are long-lasting with maintenance. Hair regrowth is reduced and becomes finer with regular sessions.
06
What’s the difference between IPL and laser hair removal?
IPL uses a broad spectrum light while laser uses a single wavelength. Both target melanin in the hair; IPL devices are typically more versatile for home use.
07
Can men use Lumeva?
Yes, men can use it on suitable body areas (avoid face/beard). Results may take longer on dense, coarse hair.

Usage & Device Care

08
Do I need to shave before using IPL?
Yes. Shave the treatment area beforehand so the light energy can target the hair root directly. Avoid waxing or plucking before your session, as IPL needs the root present for effective results.
09
How often should I use it?
For best results, use your IPL device once every two weeks for the first 4–6 sessions. After noticeable reduction, switch to monthly touch-ups or as needed to maintain smooth skin.
10
Can I use it every day?
No. Daily use isn’t necessary and may irritate the skin. IPL works on the hair growth cycle, so allowing time between sessions ensures the light effectively targets new hair regrowth.
11
What areas can I use it on?
It’s suitable for most body areas including legs, arms, underarms, and bikini line. Some models can also be used on the face below the cheekbones. Avoid use on sensitive or broken skin.
12
What should I do after treatment?
Keep the treated area clean and cool. Avoid hot showers, tight clothing, and sun exposure for 24 hours. Moisturize gently and apply sunscreen if you go outdoors.
13
How do I clean the device?
Turn off and unplug the device. Wipe the light window with a soft, lint-free cloth after every use. Avoid water or chemical cleaners to protect the internal components.
